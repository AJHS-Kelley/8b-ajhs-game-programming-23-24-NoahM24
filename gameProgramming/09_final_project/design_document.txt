Game Genre / Type: Lose all your money
Theme: Casino
Name: GYLA (Gamble Your Life Away)

Win Condition: Beat other player
Lose Condition: Get beat or fold

Levels / Screen:
    -- Casino
    -- Random Table

Objectives / Enimies / Obstacles:
    -- Beat the other player
    -- The other player is your enemy
    -- Crappy cards

Items / Equipment / Objects:
    -- The cards

Difficulty Changes:
    -- Lose more money if you lose
    -- Better AI
        -- Bigger Bets
        -- Smarter decisions

Controls:
    -- B - Bets money
    -- D - Add more to your bet
    -- F - Fold your hand (Lose)
    -- S - Stand and not bet
